iPhone/iPad

In today's society the smartphone controls our lives and the way we operate. When you look around everyone has a smartphone and they all have a similar design and features as each other. We can all thank Apple for the smartphone industry because Apple revolutionized this industry when they first came out with the original iPhone in 2007 which shocked the technology world. From 2007 - present day, Apple has created 10 different models of the iPhone. Each model is a successor to the previous with faster processing speed and improved interface and design. 2013 began the generation of iPhones that were different in sizes and models and the public was able to choose their preference. Jonathan Ive was the designer behind the iPhone while Steve Jobs focused all the attention towards this project.

Originally Apple's focus was to design a tablet, but it shifted it's focus to designing the iPhone. Once the iPhone was released and became part of Apple's product line, Apple shifted their focus back to the tablet project. Apple released a tablet computer in 1993 called the Newton MessagePad 100 but was soon discontinued. After the launch of the iPhone, Apple shifted their focus and re-entered the project to create the iPad. The iPad was announced in 2010 by Steve Jobs. Since 2010 Apple has introduced 9 different models of the iPad each improving the previous model. In today's society we have iPad mini which is a smaller version of the original but with the same functions, the original iPad but in it's 4th generation with a faster processor and more features, and the very thin iPad air which has the same functionality and features as the others but in a thinner state.
