iMac/MacBook

The iMac is an all-in-one macintosh computer that was first announced in 1998. Ken Segall, an employee at an ad agency that handled Apple's account, came up with the name iMac and Steve Jobs grew to love the name. The "i" in iMac can stand as individuality or and stand as internet, however you like to perceive it.

The iMac was the first legacy-free PC and used a USB port. After many years the iMac has become one of the most successful computers in history, and it's still improving. Just this year the new iMac with 5K display was announced!

The Macbook is a notebook computer that was manufactured by Apple in 2006. The Macbook is one of the most popular laptops we see in today's society because of its processors, great battery life, beautiful design, and innovative software that ensure the user complete satisfaction. This notebook was origanally created for the education market but soon everyone wanted their hands on it. The latest editions of Macbooks are separated into two types, Macbook Air, and Macbook Pro. The Macbook Air is a very thin model that is 19 mm thin but has create processing speed and memory. The MacBook pro is the powerhouse of the two laptops, the pro is used for major processes and many businesses are run on these laptops.

Did you know..

The first apple computer, Apple 1 in 1979, did not include any hardware features like keyboards monitors or a case, it was just a circuit board. The Apple II was introduced a year later which revolutionized the home computing industry.
